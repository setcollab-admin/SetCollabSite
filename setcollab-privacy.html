Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026. This policy explains what information SetCollab collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

What we collect

When you join the SetCollab waitlist or create an account, we may collect information including your name, email address, role in the creative industry, and any other information you provide directly. During identity verification, we may collect additional documentation as required by the verification process.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to operate the platform, send early access invitations and launch updates, verify user identities, support safety and accountability features, and improve the SetCollab service. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Waitlist communications

If you join the SetCollab waitlist, your information will be used only for early access invitations, beta updates, and launch communications. You can unsubscribe from these communications at any time by following the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. SetCollab uses standard security practices appropriate to the sensitivity of the information we handle.

Identity verification

As part of SetCollab's trust and safety model, users may be required to complete identity verification. Verification data is handled in accordance with applicable law and is used only for the purpose of confirming user identity on the platform.

Recurrence prevention

In cases where a user has been removed from the platform for conduct violations, SetCollab may retain certain identifying information for the purpose of preventing the creation of new accounts by that user. This is done to protect the safety of the community.

Third-party services

SetCollab may use third-party services to operate the platform, including form handling, payment processing, and analytics tools. These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies. SetCollab selects third-party services that meet appropriate privacy and security standards.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete information we hold about you. To make a request, contact us at the address below.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how SetCollab handles your information, you can reach us through the contact information provided on the SetCollab platform or waitlist confirmation communications.

Changes to this policy

SetCollab may update this privacy policy as the platform develops. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of the platform after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
