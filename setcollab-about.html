About SetCollab

SetCollab is building the single secure place for the full creative shoot workflow — replacing scattered tools with one connected platform built for models, photographers, makeup artists, stylists, and trusted shoot companions.

Our mission

Creative shoots already follow a real workflow. People find collaborators, confirm details, handle paperwork, manage safety, and settle payment — but all of that happens across Instagram, DMs, email threads, separate document tools, and payment apps. SetCollab is being built to bring that entire process into one secure place.

Our mission is to make every creative shoot safer, more organized, and more professional by giving everyone involved a shared system they can trust.

What we stand for

Trust through verification

Every profile on SetCollab is verified. Knowing who you are working with before the shoot starts is a foundation, not a feature.

Clarity before the shoot

Agreements, roles, deliverables, and expectations should be confirmed before anyone steps on set. SetCollab builds that into the workflow.

Safety built into the process

Safety is not an afterthought. Set Buddy support, verified locations, reporting tools, and accountability features are part of the platform itself.

Accountability after the shoot

Payment, ratings, and follow-through happen in the same system — so professional standards apply from first contact to final delivery.

Who SetCollab is for

SetCollab is designed for the people who make creative shoots happen: models, photographers, makeup artists, stylists, and trusted shoot companions. Whether someone is building a first portfolio or running shoots regularly, the platform is designed to make collaboration clearer, safer, and more dependable.
