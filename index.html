Built for models, photographers, makeup artists, stylists, and trusted shoot companions who want a more professional way to work together.

The workflow already exists. The problem is where it lives.

Right now, creative teams piece together Instagram, DMs, email, documents, and payment tools just to get through one shoot. Details get lost, expectations stay vague, and too much depends on informal processes instead of a shared system.

  • No clear place to align on roles, deliverables, and usage rights.
  • No consistent structure for trust, accountability, and follow-through.
  • Too much coordination scattered across disconnected tools.

What SetCollab is building

SetCollab is a platform designed to bring the full shoot workflow into one secure place — so everyone involved can find collaborators, confirm expectations, complete required paperwork, manage safety, and handle payment without switching tools.

Not an open marketplace — a verified creative network.
