Trust & Safety

Safety on SetCollab is not a separate feature — it is built directly into the shoot workflow. Every step of the process is designed to help creative teams work with more confidence, accountability, and protection.

How trust works on SetCollab

Verified profiles

Every SetCollab account goes through a verification process. Before connecting with anyone for a shoot, you can see that their profile has been verified — so you know who you are working with before anything else begins.

Pre-shoot agreements

SetCollab requires agreements to be completed before the shoot starts. These cover deliverables, usage rights, timing, roles, and expectations so everyone is aligned on the same terms before anyone steps on set. Agreements are documented inside the platform as part of the shoot record.

Set Buddy support

A Set Buddy is a trusted companion who can be added to a shoot through SetCollab. Their presence is verified, documented, and part of the official shoot record. Set Buddy check-ins are built into the workflow so their involvement is tracked rather than informal.

Verified locations

Shoot locations can be verified through SetCollab before the shoot takes place. This gives everyone involved confirmation that the location is legitimate and on record as part of the shoot documentation.

Blocking and reporting

SetCollab includes tools to block other users and report conduct that violates platform standards. Reports are handled through a structured process so that issues are documented and addressed rather than ignored.

Post-shoot ratings

After a shoot is completed, all parties can leave ratings. Ratings create an ongoing accountability record for everyone on the platform — which helps maintain professional standards and gives future collaborators more context before they connect.

Our commitment

SetCollab is being built on the belief that trust and safety belong inside the workflow, not outside of it. The platform is designed so that verification, agreements, companion support, location confirmation, and accountability are standard parts of every shoot — not optional extras.

We are committed to continuing to develop these features based on the needs of the creative community, and to maintaining a platform where professional collaboration can happen with more confidence on all sides.
