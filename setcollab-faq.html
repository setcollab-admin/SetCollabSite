Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about SetCollab, how it works, and what to expect during early access.

About SetCollab

What is SetCollab?

SetCollab is a platform designed to bring the full creative shoot workflow into one secure place. Instead of piecing together Instagram, DMs, email, document tools, and payment apps, SetCollab gives everyone involved in a shoot one shared system for finding collaborators, confirming details, completing required paperwork, managing safety, and handling final payment.

Who is SetCollab for?

SetCollab is designed for models, photographers, makeup artists, stylists, and trusted shoot companions. Whether you are building your first portfolio or managing frequent professional shoots, the platform is built to make the process clearer, safer, and more dependable.

Is SetCollab an open marketplace?

No. SetCollab is a verified creative network, not an open marketplace. Profiles are verified, and the platform is designed around structured workflows rather than open public listings. That distinction matters for trust, accountability, and the overall quality of collaboration.

What problem does SetCollab solve?

Right now, creative shoot workflows happen across many disconnected tools. People find collaborators on Instagram, coordinate over DMs, handle paperwork through email or separate document apps, and settle payment through PayPal or other apps. SetCollab brings all of that into one connected workflow so nothing gets lost and the process stays professional from start to finish.

How It Works

What does the SetCollab workflow look like?

The workflow follows five steps: find collaborators, confirm the shoot, complete required paperwork, manage safety and accountability, and handle final payment. All five steps happen inside SetCollab so you do not need to jump between tools to get through a shoot.

What kind of paperwork does SetCollab handle?

SetCollab supports the agreements and approvals that shoots require before work begins — including model releases, usage rights, deliverable expectations, and role confirmations. The goal is to make sure everyone is aligned before anyone steps on set.

How does payment work on SetCollab?

Payment is handled inside the platform as part of the post-shoot workflow. The goal is to move from completed shoot to payment without switching to a separate app, keeping the full process in one place.

Trust and Safety

How does SetCollab handle safety?

Safety is built into the workflow itself, not added as an afterthought. SetCollab includes verified profiles, pre-shoot agreements, Set Buddy support, verified location confirmation, blocking and reporting tools, and post-shoot ratings. Each of those is part of the standard shoot process on the platform.

What is a Set Buddy?

A Set Buddy is a trusted companion who can be brought into the shoot workflow through SetCollab. The platform includes structured verification and check-in features for Set Buddies so their presence is documented and part of the official shoot record.

What happens if something goes wrong during a shoot?

SetCollab includes reporting tools so users can flag issues directly within the platform. Post-shoot ratings also create an ongoing accountability record that helps the community maintain professional standards over time.
